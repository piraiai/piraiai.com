‚ùå DON'Ts - Avoid These

Browser Actions to Avoid

  • ‚ùå DON'T press F5 or browser refresh - you'll lose all progress and need to log in again
  • ‚ùå DON'T use browser back/forward buttons - use app navigation in sidebar instead
  • ‚ùå DON'T close the tab before downloading your converted files
  • ‚ùå DON'T open multiple Pirai AI tabs - work in one tab only
 

Session Management

  • ‚ùå DON'T expect your session to persist after browser refresh
  • ‚ùå DON'T leave conversions incomplete - uploaded files won't survive refresh
  • ‚ùå DON'T assume auto-save - manually download all important files
  • ‚ùå DON'T share your session URL - each user needs their own login
‚ö†Ô∏è Critical Streamlit Behavior

Streamlit apps are stateful within a session but stateless across browser refreshes.

What this means: Your work persists as long as browser tab stays open. Pressing F5 or closing tab = all progress lost.

Best Practice: Treat each work session as temporary and download results promptly!

Common Issues & Solutions

"Conversion missed some questions or content"

Solution:
  • Try different extraction method in Step 1
  • For PDFs with images or scans: Use "PDF Advanced"
  • For complex formatting: Use "Visual Layout Preserved"
  • For simple docs that failed: Try "Alternative Parser"

"File too large" error

Solution:
  • Remove images/graphics from document
  • Compress using "Save As ‚Üí Tools ‚Üí Compress Pictures"
  • Split into multiple smaller files
  • Use multi-file merge feature in Step 2

FAQ Quick Reference

Q: Can I convert one survey to multiple platforms?

A: Yes! Upload once in Step 1, then convert to as many platforms as needed in Step 2.

Q: Can I migrate surveys between platforms?

A: Absolutely! Upload a platform export file (QSF, XML, MDD, etc.) in Step 1 to convert it to Excel, then convert to any target platform in Step 2. Perfect for platform migration projects.

Q: Does Pirai AI store my survey data?

A: No. Files are processed in memory and automatically deleted. GDPR compliant.

Q: What if my document has typos?

A: Download the typo report from Step 1, fix in Excel, then re-upload to Step 2.

Q: Can I edit the survey after conversion?

A: Yes, use AI Editor before conversion or edit manually in the downloaded platform file.

Q: How accurate is the AI conversion?

A: Highly accurate with automatic quality validation. Always review the preview before finalizing.
