REDCap Converter Questions

How do I convert a Word document to REDCap?

Upload your Word document to platform.piraiai.com, select REDCap, review the converted survey, and click Finalize and Download. In REDCap, go to Project Setup > Data Dictionary > Upload File, select your CSV, and click Commit Changes.

What is a REDCap Data Dictionary?

A REDCap Data Dictionary is a CSV file that defines the structure of a REDCap project. It includes field names, field types (radio, checkbox, text, notes, dropdown, descriptive, calc), field labels, answer choices, and validation rules. Pirai AI generates a fully populated Data Dictionary CSV from any source document.

Can I convert a scanned paper CRF to REDCap?

Yes. Scan your paper Case Report Form and save as PDF. Upload to platform.piraiai.com with Advanced PDF Processing enabled. Pirai AI extracts all fields from the image-based PDF and generates a REDCap Data Dictionary CSV.

Can I convert a Qualtrics QSF file to REDCap?

Yes. Upload your QSF file to platform.piraiai.com, select REDCap, and download the Data Dictionary CSV. Pirai AI maps Qualtrics question types to REDCap field types: MC:SingleAnswer to radio, MC:MultipleAnswer to checkbox, TE:SingleLine to text, TE:Essay to notes, MC:Dropdown to dropdown.

All answer choices formatted as pipe-separated code, label pairs (e.g. 1, Yes | 2, No | 3, Not sure).
