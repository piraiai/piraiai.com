What Flow Can Do

Everything you need to configure a Qualtrics dashboard, through conversation

Create Widgets

Describe any chart in plain English and Flow configures and creates it instantly: number charts, bar charts, line charts, breakdown bars, donut charts, tables, and word clouds.

Add Page Filters

Tell Flow which filters you need. Flow creates and applies them to your dashboard page in one step: date ranges, NPS scores, response types, distribution channels, and more.

Clone Pages

Copy entire dashboard pages across dashboards with a single instruction. All widgets, filters, and layouts are transferred and reconfigured for the target dashboard automatically.

Ask Your Dashboard

Query your dashboard structure in plain English. Page counts, widget summaries, filter lists. Flow knows your dashboard inside out and answers instantly.

Bulk Widget Creation

Describe multiple widgets at once. Flow recommends a complete set, you confirm with one click, and it builds all of them, with progress tracked in real time.

Intelligent Field Mapping

Flow understands your available fields and their types. It automatically selects the right field for every widget, with no manual field lookup and no configuration errors.
