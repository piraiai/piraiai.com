About Pirai AI

Giving people their time back through intelligent automation

Our Mission

At Pirai AI, we believe human intelligence is too valuable to waste on manual data entry. Our mission is simple: eliminate the tedious work that keeps you from focusing on what truly matters.

Every day, millions of professionals across industries spend hours manually entering data, formatting documents, and converting between platforms. This isn't just inefficient—it's a fundamental misallocation of human potential.

We're building AI automation tools that give you your time back.

Why "Pirai"?

Pirai means "crescent moon" in Tamil, symbolizing new beginnings, growth, and the elegant curve of progress. Just as the moon illuminates the darkness, we illuminate the path to intelligent automation across all platforms.

After delivering 150+ voice-of-customer programs across retail, telecommunications, government, education, and healthcare during my 15-year career at Ipsos, I recognised a common challenge: highly skilled professionals were spending countless hours manually converting survey documents, rebuilding surveys across platforms, and configuring dashboards instead of focusing on research and decision-making.

I founded Pirai AI to eliminate this repetitive work through AI-powered automation. Today, our platform converts research documents into survey formats across 25+ leading survey platforms, while Pirai AI Flow automates dashboard development and configuration workflows. Together, these products significantly reduce manual effort and accelerate survey and analytics delivery.

Since launch, Pirai AI has processed more than 6,000 survey documents, converted over one million survey questions and answer options, and used more than 300 million AI tokens to power intelligent document understanding and automation. Our platform is trusted by researchers, universities, government organisations, healthcare providers, nonprofits, and enterprises in 60+ countries, helping teams spend less time on manual survey programming and more time generating meaningful insights.

With over 15 years of experience as a Qualtrics-certified XM Solution Architect and full-stack software engineer, I specialise in applying generative AI to automate complex research workflows, survey migration, and enterprise survey operations. My focus is building practical AI products that solve real business problems and deliver measurable productivity gains.
