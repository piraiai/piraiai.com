Our Data Protection Promise

Your survey documents are processed in memory and are never written to persistent storage. Files are cleared automatically once your session ends, and only limited metadata is retained for operational purposes.

Secure Upload
Encrypted transmission for every file you send
In-Memory Processing
AI conversion happens without writing files to disk
Secure Delivery
Your converted file is returned over an encrypted connection
Automatic Deletion
Files are cleared automatically once your session ends

Document Content

Never written to disk; cleared automatically once processing completes

Processing Metadata

Only limited operational data is retained (file size, status, timestamps)

AI Training

Your documents and survey content are never used to train AI models

Third-Party Access

Your data is never shared with third parties beyond processing your request

Security Capabilities

Layered protection covering access, data handling, and infrastructure

Account Security & Access Control

Enterprise-grade authentication protecting every user session.

  • Encrypted, token-based session management
  • Automatic session timeouts and lockout after repeated failed logins
  • Role-based access controls

Data Privacy & Minimization

We store as little as possible, and never your survey content.

  • No survey content, question text, or file content stored in our database
  • Only minimal metadata retained for operations and support
  • Email addresses stored as one-way hashes, never in plain text

Encryption

Your data is encrypted both moving and at rest.

  • TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • Encrypted storage for any retained metadata
  • No document content ever stored at rest

Input Validation & Threat Prevention

Comprehensive checks stop malicious files and inputs before they reach processing.

  • File type and size validation on every upload
  • Automated bot protection
  • Multi-layer sanitization against injection attacks

Monitoring & Incident Response

Issues are caught early and resolved quickly.

  • Continuous system and error monitoring
  • Defined incident response process
  • Automated backups with rapid recovery

Vendor & Infrastructure Security

Every part of our stack is vetted to the same standard we hold ourselves to.

  • Infrastructure providers assessed for SOC 2, ISO 27001, and GDPR alignment
  • Access-controlled repositories with automated vulnerability scanning
  • Regular security patch cycles

Your Data Rights

We respect your privacy rights and give you full control over your personal data
