How it works

Word to Google Forms in 3 Steps

No add-on to install. No Drive permissions to grant. The Apps Script editor is built into every Google Form.

1

Upload your Word document

Go to platform.piraiai.com. Upload your Word (.docx) file. Select Google Forms as the output. Pirai AI reads your Word document, extracts all questions, answer options, numbering, and table-based matrix grids, then generates an Apps Script.

2

Open Apps Script in Google Forms

Open a blank Google Form at forms.google.com. Click the three-dot menu in the top right. Select Apps Script. The built-in code editor opens. Delete the default placeholder code. Paste your Pirai AI script.

3

Save, run, done

Click Save (floppy disk icon). Click Run. Google shows a one-time permissions prompt : click Review permissions > Allow. The script runs, creates all your questions automatically, and your Google Form is complete.

The one-time permissions prompt is a standard Google safety check. The script only accesses the specific form you opened. It does not touch your Drive, Gmail, or any other Google service. Full explanation here.

FAQ

Word to Google Forms Converter Questions

How do I convert a Word document to Google Forms?

Upload your Word (.docx) file to platform.piraiai.com, select Google Forms as the output, and download the generated Apps Script .txt file. Open a blank Google Form, click the three-dot menu > Apps Script, paste the script, click Save then Run. Grant the one-time Google permission and your form is built automatically with all questions and answer options from your Word document.

Does the Word to Google Forms converter preserve question formatting?

Pirai AI preserves the structure of your Word document: question text, answer options, numbering, question types (multiple choice, checkboxes, short answer, etc.), and table-based matrix grids. Bold and italic text formatting within questions is not carried over since Google Forms has limited rich text support. Images embedded in Word documents are not transferred to Google Forms.

Can I convert a Word document with Likert scale tables to Google Forms?

Yes. Pirai AI recognizes Likert scale and matrix grid tables in Word documents and converts them to Multiple Choice Grid or Checkbox Grid question types in Google Forms. This is one of the most common question types in academic surveys, and most Workspace add-ons fail to convert these correctly.

Does Pirai AI require a Google Workspace add-on?

No. Pirai AI generates an Apps Script file. The Apps Script editor is a built-in feature of every Google Form : click the three-dot menu in the top right of any Google Form to access it. No Marketplace installation required. No Drive permissions granted to any third party.

What if my Word file is in the older .doc format?

Pirai AI accepts .docx files (Microsoft Word 2007 and later format). If you have an older .doc file, open it in Microsoft Word or Google Docs and save it as .docx first. Alternatively, you can copy-paste the text content from your Word document directly into Pirai AI's text input field.

Is Pirai AI Google Forms converter free?

Yes. Pirai AI gives you 1-2 free credits every day that reset daily. Each Word to Google Forms conversion uses one credit. Credit packs are available: 5 credits for $2.99, 15 credits for $6.99, 30 credits for $9.99. Credits never expire.
