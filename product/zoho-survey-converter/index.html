Zoho Survey Converter Questions

How do I convert a Word document to Zoho Survey?

Upload your Word document to platform.piraiai.com, select Zoho Survey as the output platform, review the converted survey in the workspace, and download the generated .docx or .txt file. In Zoho Survey, click Create Survey, select Import Survey, choose Import Document, and upload the file. Zoho Survey auto-detects question types and builds the survey.

Does Zoho Survey support direct Word document import?

Yes. Zoho Survey accepts .docx and .txt files directly through Import Survey > Import Document, and automatically assigns question types and populates answer options on upload. Pirai AI generates a correctly formatted file so detection is accurate on the first import.

What is the difference between Import Document and Bulk Question Builder?

Import Document is used when creating a new survey and builds the full survey from an uploaded file. Bulk Question Builder is used inside an existing survey's editor to paste formatted text and add questions in real time, without starting a new survey.

Can I convert a Qualtrics QSF file to Zoho Survey?

Yes. Upload your QSF file to platform.piraiai.com, select Zoho Survey, and download the converted file. Pirai AI maps Qualtrics question types (MC:SingleAnswer, MC:MultipleAnswer, Matrix, TE:Essay etc.) to their Zoho Survey equivalents automatically.

Can I also convert to other platforms from the same upload?

Yes. From your Conversion History, click Download As on any completed conversion and select any of 26 supported platforms including Qualtrics, SurveyMonkey, REDCap, Google Forms, Microsoft Forms, Forsta, and more. No reprocessing required.
