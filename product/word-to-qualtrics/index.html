Word to Qualtrics Conversion Questions

How do I convert a Word document to Qualtrics?

Upload your Word (.docx) file to platform.piraiai.com, select Qualtrics as the output platform, and Pirai AI will convert your document to a structured survey. Review each question in the workspace using Smart Fix Assistant or AI Survey Editor, then click Finalize and Download. Import the QSF file into Qualtrics via Survey > Import Survey. The entire process takes under 10 minutes.

Does Pirai AI support skip logic and display logic?

Yes. Pirai AI reads skip logic and display logic instructions from your Word document and maps them to Qualtrics skip logic and display logic in the generated QSF file. Write conditions in plain text near the relevant question, such as "If Q1 = Yes, skip to Q5" or "Show Q3 only if Q2 = Other." Pirai AI converts these instructions to valid Qualtrics logic.

What formatting tips should I follow in my Word document?

For best results: use numbered or bulleted lists for questions and answer options. Use tables for matrix questions. Include labels like "select one" or "select all that apply" to help with question type detection. Write skip logic and display logic as plain-text instructions near the relevant question. Bold your question text and indent answer options. Pirai AI handles most Word formatting automatically, but clear structure produces the best conversion.
