How it works

Google Doc to Google Forms in 3 Steps

No Doc2Form or Automagical Forms add-on to install. Export your Google Doc as Word, upload, and run the script.

1

Export your Google Doc as Word (.docx) and upload

Open your Google Doc. Click File > Download > Microsoft Word (.docx). Go to platform.piraiai.com and upload the downloaded .docx file. Select Google Forms as the output. Pirai AI reads the document and generates an Apps Script with all questions pre-populated.

2

Open Apps Script in Google Forms

Open a blank Google Form at forms.google.com. Click the three-dot menu in the top right. Select Apps Script. The built-in code editor opens. Delete the default placeholder code. Paste your Pirai AI script.

3

Save, run, done

Click Save (floppy disk icon). Click Run. Google shows a one-time permissions prompt : click Review permissions > Allow. The script runs, creates all your questions automatically, and your Google Form is complete.

The one-time permissions prompt is a standard Google safety check. The script only accesses the specific form you opened. It does not touch your Drive, Gmail, or any other Google service. Full explanation here.

FAQ

Google Doc to Google Forms Converter Questions

How do I convert a Google Doc to Google Forms?

Open your Google Doc and click File > Download > Microsoft Word (.docx) to export it. Upload the .docx file to platform.piraiai.com, select Google Forms, and download the Apps Script .txt file. Open a blank Google Form, click three-dot menu > Apps Script, paste the script, click Save then Run. Your form is built automatically from your Google Doc content.

Why do I need to export my Google Doc as Word first?

Google Docs are stored in Google's proprietary cloud format and cannot be directly downloaded as a file to upload elsewhere. Exporting as Word (.docx) takes 2 seconds (File > Download > Microsoft Word) and creates a standard file that preserves all your text, formatting, tables, and structure. Pirai AI then processes this .docx file to extract your questions and answer options.

Is this better than installing Doc2Form or Automagical Forms?

Doc2Form and Automagical Forms are Workspace Marketplace add-ons that require installation and grant ongoing access to your Google Drive. They often have question limits on free tiers and fail on matrix grid questions. Pirai AI generates an Apps Script instead: no installation, no Drive permissions, no question limits, supports matrix grids, and converts to 26 other platforms from the same upload.

Does Pirai AI require a Google Workspace add-on?

No. Pirai AI generates an Apps Script file. The Apps Script editor is a built-in feature of every Google Form : click the three-dot menu in the top right of any Google Form to access it. No Marketplace installation required. No Drive permissions granted to any third party.

Can I convert a Google Doc with tables and Likert scales to Google Forms?

Yes. When you export your Google Doc as Word (.docx), all tables are preserved in the export. Pirai AI recognizes Likert scale and matrix grid tables and converts them to Multiple Choice Grid or Checkbox Grid question types in Google Forms. This is a significant advantage over Workspace add-ons that typically cannot handle grid questions.
