FAQ

QuestionPro Converter Questions

How do I convert a Word document to QuestionPro?

Upload your Word document to platform.piraiai.com, select QuestionPro as the output platform, review the converted survey in the workspace, and download the generated .docx file. In QuestionPro, create a new blank survey, expand the Add Question menu, select Add from MS Word, and import the file. All questions are created automatically.

Why won't my Word document import into QuestionPro?

QuestionPro's Add from MS Word feature only accepts .docx files structured to its specific import template. A standard Word document with survey questions will fail or produce incorrect results. Pirai AI generates a .docx file already formatted to QuestionPro's exact specification.

Is Add from MS Word available on all QuestionPro surveys?

No. Add from MS Word is only available on new, blank surveys. It cannot be used to add questions to a survey that already has content. Always create a new empty survey before importing your Pirai AI file.

Can I convert a Qualtrics QSF file to QuestionPro?

Yes. Upload your QSF file to platform.piraiai.com, select QuestionPro, and download the converted .docx file. Pirai AI maps Qualtrics question types (MC:SingleAnswer, MC:MultipleAnswer, Matrix, TE:Essay etc.) to their QuestionPro equivalents automatically.

Can I also convert to other platforms from the same upload?

Yes. From your Conversion History, click Download As on any completed conversion and select any of 26 supported platforms including Qualtrics, SurveyMonkey, REDCap, Google Forms, Microsoft Forms, Forsta, and more. No reprocessing required.
