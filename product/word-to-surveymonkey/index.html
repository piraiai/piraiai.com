FAQ

Word to SurveyMonkey Questions

How do I convert a Word document to SurveyMonkey?

SurveyMonkey does not support direct Word file upload. Upload your Word document to platform.piraiai.com, select SurveyMonkey, and download the .txt file. Open the file, select all, copy, and paste into SurveyMonkey Create Survey > Paste Your Content. Check the Preview panel and click Add Questions.

Can I upload a Word document directly to SurveyMonkey?

No. SurveyMonkey does not support direct Word (.docx) file upload as an import method. The only supported import method is Paste Your Content, where you paste text into the Import Questions screen. Pirai AI converts your Word document to correctly formatted text for this feature.

Why not just copy-paste directly from my Word document?

Pasting directly from Word into SurveyMonkey often fails because Word documents contain hidden formatting metadata (styles, track changes, XML tags, custom numbering) that confuses the SurveyMonkey import AI. Pirai AI strips all formatting noise and generates clean structured text that SurveyMonkey reads accurately.

What question types does SurveyMonkey detect from the pasted text?

SurveyMonkey Paste Your Content natively recognises Multiple Choice and Single Textbox. The AI-powered version (powered by OpenAI and Azure OpenAI) detects additional types including Checkboxes, Comment Box, Matrix, Rating Scale, Ranking, and Dropdown. Pirai AI structures the text to maximise accurate detection.

Can I edit question types after importing into SurveyMonkey?

Yes. After importing via Paste Your Content, all question types can be edited and switched inside the SurveyMonkey designer. You can change question types, add logic, edit answer options, add page breaks, and fully customise your survey.
