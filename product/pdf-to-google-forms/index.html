How it works

PDF to Google Forms in 3 Steps

No add-on to install. No Drive permissions to grant. Works with both native and scanned PDFs.

1

Upload your PDF file

Go to platform.piraiai.com. Upload your PDF file. For scanned PDFs or photographed surveys, enable Advanced Processing to use OCR text extraction. Select Google Forms as the output. Pirai AI reads your PDF and generates an Apps Script with all questions pre-populated.

2

Open Apps Script in Google Forms

Open a blank Google Form at forms.google.com. Click the three-dot menu in the top right. Select Apps Script. The built-in code editor opens. Delete the default placeholder code. Paste your Pirai AI script.

3

Save, run, done

Click Save (floppy disk icon). Click Run. Google shows a one-time permissions prompt : click Review permissions > Allow. The script runs, creates all your questions automatically, and your Google Form is complete.

The one-time permissions prompt is a standard Google safety check. The script only accesses the specific form you opened. It does not touch your Drive, Gmail, or any other Google service. Full explanation here.

FAQ

PDF to Google Forms Converter Questions

How do I convert a PDF to Google Forms?

Upload your PDF file to platform.piraiai.com, select Google Forms as the output, and download the generated Apps Script .txt file. For scanned PDFs, enable Advanced Processing. Then open a blank Google Form, click the three-dot menu > Apps Script, paste the script, click Save then Run. Your form is built automatically from your PDF.

Can I convert a scanned PDF survey to Google Forms?

Yes. Pirai AI supports scanned PDF documents through Advanced PDF Processing, which uses OCR to extract text from scanned pages. This works for scanned paper surveys, photographed questionnaires, and image-based PDFs. Enable the Advanced Processing toggle when uploading your scanned PDF for best results.

Does the PDF converter handle multi-page survey PDFs?

Yes. Pirai AI processes all pages of your PDF file. Multi-page surveys, questionnaires, and exam papers are fully supported. The generated Apps Script includes every question from every page. There is no page limit on how many pages your PDF can contain.

Does Pirai AI require a Google Workspace add-on?

No. Pirai AI generates an Apps Script file. The Apps Script editor is a built-in feature of every Google Form : click the three-dot menu in the top right of any Google Form to access it. No Marketplace installation required. No Drive permissions granted to any third party.

What about password-protected PDFs?

Password-protected PDFs must be unlocked before uploading to Pirai AI. Open the PDF in your PDF reader, enter the password, and save an unlocked copy. Then upload the unlocked PDF to Pirai AI. Standard (non-protected) PDF files can be uploaded directly without any preparation.

Is Pirai AI Google Forms converter free?

Yes. Pirai AI gives you 1-2 free credits every day that reset daily. Each PDF to Google Forms conversion uses one credit (Advanced Processing for scanned PDFs may use additional credits). Credit packs: 5 credits for $2.99, 15 credits for $6.99, 30 credits for $9.99. Credits never expire.
