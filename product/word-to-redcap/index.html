How do I convert a Word document to REDCap?

Upload your Word document (.docx) to platform.piraiai.com, select REDCap as the output platform, review the converted survey in the workspace, and click Finalize and Download. In REDCap, go to Project Setup > Data Dictionary > Upload File, select your CSV, and click Commit Changes.

What REDCap field types does Pirai AI generate from a Word document?

Pirai AI generates all standard REDCap field types from Word documents: radio (single choice), checkbox (multiple choice), text (short answer), notes (long text), dropdown (single choice dropdown), descriptive (text display), calc (calculated field placeholder), and file (file upload). All field types are assigned automatically based on the structure of your Word document.

Does Pirai AI preserve the structure of my Word questionnaire?

Yes. Pirai AI reads headings, numbered lists, tables, and formatting in your Word document to identify sections, questions, and answer options. It maps these to REDCap instrument structure with correct form names, section headers, and field groupings. The converted Data Dictionary preserves the logical flow of your original Word document.

Can I convert a Word-based CRF to REDCap?

Yes. Upload your Word-based Case Report Form to platform.piraiai.com and select REDCap. Pirai AI extracts all fields, assigns REDCap field types, formats answer choices with pipe-separated codes, and generates a complete Data Dictionary CSV ready for upload into REDCap.

Does the converter handle validation rules and branching logic?

Pirai AI populates all required Data Dictionary columns including text validation type, text validation min and max, branching logic, and required field markers. You can review and edit validation rules and branching logic in the workspace before downloading the final CSV.
