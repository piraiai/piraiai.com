Can I convert a scanned paper CRF to REDCap?

Yes. Scan your paper Case Report Form and save as PDF. Upload to platform.piraiai.com with Advanced PDF Processing enabled. What is Advanced PDF Processing?

Advanced PDF Processing is a Pirai AI feature that uses AI vision to extract fields from image-based and scanned PDF documents. Standard text extraction cannot read scanned paper forms. Advanced PDF Processing reads checkboxes, radio buttons, text fields, tables, and section headers from scanned images and converts them to structured data.

Does Pirai AI handle both native and scanned PDFs?

Yes. For native PDFs (digitally created), Pirai AI extracts text and structure directly. For scanned PDFs and image-based documents, enable Advanced PDF Processing. Pirai AI uses AI vision to identify fields, answer options, and document structure from the scanned images.

What REDCap field types does Pirai AI generate from a PDF?

Pirai AI generates all standard REDCap field types from PDFs: radio (single choice), checkbox (multiple choice), text (short answer), notes (long text), dropdown (single choice dropdown), descriptive (text display), calc (calculated field placeholder), and file (file upload). All field types are assigned automatically based on the content extracted from your PDF.
