Forsta to Qualtrics Migration Questions

How do I migrate a survey from Forsta to Qualtrics?

Export your survey from Forsta as bConvert, Word, or XML, or use your original source document. Upload it to platform.piraiai.com, select Qualtrics as the output platform, choose QSF format, review the converted survey in the workspace, and download the .qsf file. In Qualtrics, go to the Survey tab, click Tools, select Import Survey, and upload the file.

Can I convert a Forsta survey directly to a Qualtrics QSF file?

Yes. Pirai AI accepts your Forsta export or original source document and generates a Qualtrics QSF file with the full survey structure. Pirai AI maps Forsta question types, including matrix and grid questions, to their Qualtrics equivalents automatically.

Do I need my Forsta export, or can I use the original document?

Either works. If you still have portal access, exporting from Forsta as bConvert, Word, or XML gives Pirai AI the most structured source. If you no longer have access, your original questionnaire document, spec, or even a scanned paper form works just as well.

Will my display logic and survey flow carry over from Forsta?

Pirai AI converts questions, answer options, blocks, and recode values into the Qualtrics QSF structure. Display logic, skip logic, and survey flow are platform-specific and are typically added or reconfigured in Qualtrics after import, since the underlying logic engines differ between platforms.

Can I migrate to a platform other than Qualtrics from the same Forsta export?

Yes. From your Conversion History, click Download As on any completed conversion and select any of 26 supported platforms including SurveyMonkey, REDCap, Google Forms, Microsoft Forms, QuestionPro, and more. No reprocessing required.

Pirai AI converts Forsta survey exports (bConvert .txt, Word .docx, Forsta XML .xml) or original source documents (Word, PDF, Excel, CSV, plain text, XML, JSON) into a Qualtrics QSF file. Qualtrics accepts two import formats: QSF (.qsf), which carries the full survey structure including blocks, display logic, embedded data, and survey flow, and Advanced Format (.txt or .docx), a simpler text specification typically used to append questions to an existing survey. Import path: Qualtrics Survey tab > Tools > Import Survey > Choose File > select the .qsf file > Import.
