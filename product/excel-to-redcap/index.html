Excel to REDCap Questions

How do I convert an Excel spreadsheet to REDCap?

Upload your Excel file (.xlsx) to platform.piraiai.com, select REDCap as the output platform, review the converted survey, and click Finalize and Download. In REDCap, go to Project Setup > Data Dictionary > Upload File, select your CSV, and click Commit Changes.

What Excel formats does Pirai AI support for REDCap conversion?

Pirai AI supports .xlsx (Excel) files. It reads questions from rows and columns, identifies answer options, and maps them to REDCap field types. Excel question banks with questions in rows and answer options in columns are converted automatically. CSV files are also supported as a separate input format.

Can I convert an Excel question bank to REDCap?

Yes. Upload your Excel question bank to platform.piraiai.com and select REDCap. Pirai AI reads questions from rows, identifies answer options from adjacent columns or cells, assigns REDCap field types, and generates a complete Data Dictionary CSV with all required columns populated.

What is the difference between uploading Excel vs CSV to Pirai AI?

Both Excel (.xlsx) and CSV files are supported as input formats. Excel files preserve cell formatting, multiple columns, and richer structure. CSV files are plain text with comma-separated values. Both produce the same REDCap Data Dictionary CSV output with all required columns populated.

Can I export the converted survey back to Excel for review?

Yes. Pirai AI supports Bulk Excel export and re-import. After conversion, you can export the survey to Excel for team review, make changes in the spreadsheet, and re-import it back into Pirai AI with validation. Then download the final REDCap Data Dictionary CSV.
