How it works

Image to Google Forms in 3 Steps

No add-on to install. No Drive permissions to grant. Just snap a photo, upload, and run the script.

1

Upload a photo or scan of your exam paper

Go to platform.piraiai.com. Upload a clear photo or scanned image of your printed exam paper, quiz sheet, or paper survey. Enable Advanced Processing for OCR text extraction. Select Google Forms as the output. Pirai AI extracts all questions and generates an Apps Script.

2

Open Apps Script in Google Forms

Open a blank Google Form at forms.google.com. Click the three-dot menu in the top right. Select Apps Script. The built-in code editor opens. Delete the default placeholder code. Paste your Pirai AI script.

3

Save, run, done

Click Save (floppy disk icon). Click Run. Google shows a one-time permissions prompt : click Review permissions > Allow. The script runs, creates all your questions automatically, and your Google Form is complete.

The one-time permissions prompt is a standard Google safety check. The script only accesses the specific form you opened. It does not touch your Drive, Gmail, or any other Google service. Full explanation here.

FAQ

Image to Google Forms Converter Questions

How do I convert an image of an exam paper to Google Forms?

Take a clear photo or scan of your exam paper. Upload the image (JPG, PNG, or HEIC) to platform.piraiai.com, enable Advanced Processing for OCR, and select Google Forms. Download the generated Apps Script .txt file. Open a blank Google Form, click three-dot menu > Apps Script, paste the script, click Save then Run. Your form is built automatically from the exam paper image.

Can I just snap a photo with my phone and convert it?

Yes. Take a clear, well-lit photo of your printed exam paper or quiz with your smartphone camera. Make sure the text is readable and the image is not blurry. Upload the photo to Pirai AI with Advanced Processing enabled. The OCR engine extracts questions and answer options from the image and generates a Google Forms Apps Script.

What tips help get the best OCR results from image conversion?

For the best image to Google Forms results: use good lighting and avoid shadows on the paper, keep the camera parallel to the page to minimize skew, ensure all text is sharp and legible, and use Advanced Processing. Typed or printed text converts more accurately than handwritten text. If your exam has multiple pages, you can combine them into a PDF before uploading.

Does Pirai AI require a Google Workspace add-on?

No. Pirai AI generates an Apps Script file. The Apps Script editor is a built-in feature of every Google Form : click the three-dot menu in the top right of any Google Form to access it. No Marketplace installation required. No Drive permissions granted to any third party.

What image formats does Pirai AI accept?

Pirai AI accepts JPG, JPEG, PNG, and HEIC image formats for direct upload. You can also combine multiple images into a single PDF before uploading. For multi-page exams, creating a PDF from your images is recommended so all pages are processed together in a single conversion.

Is Pirai AI Google Forms converter free?

Yes. Pirai AI gives you 1-2 free credits every day that reset daily. Image to Google Forms conversions using Advanced Processing may use additional credits for OCR extraction. Credit packs: 5 credits for $2.99, 15 credits for $6.99, 30 credits for $9.99. Credits never expire.
