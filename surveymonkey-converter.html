How it works

From Document to SurveyMonkey in 3 Steps

Pirai AI handles the formatting. SurveyMonkey handles the import. You paste once and review.

1

Upload and convert

Go to platform.piraiai.com. Upload your file or paste text. Select SurveyMonkey as the output platform. For scanned paper surveys, enable Advanced PDF Processing. Pirai AI generates correctly formatted text for SurveyMonkey Paste Your Content.

2

Download and copy the .txt file

Download the Pirai AI .txt file. Open it, select all the content, and copy it. Review the converted survey in the workspace first if needed — use Smart Fix, AI Survey Editor, or Table View to make changes before downloading.

3

Paste into SurveyMonkey

In SurveyMonkey, click Create Survey and select Paste Your Content. On the Import Questions screen, paste your copied content. Use the Preview section on the right to check how questions are being recognised in real time. Click Add Questions. You can then give the survey a title, edit question types, and customise as needed.

FAQ

SurveyMonkey Converter Questions

How do I import a Word document into SurveyMonkey?

SurveyMonkey does not support direct Word file upload. Upload your Word document to platform.piraiai.com, select SurveyMonkey, and download the .txt file. Open the file, select all, and copy the content. In SurveyMonkey, click Create Survey > Paste Your Content. Paste into the Import Questions screen, check the real-time Preview, and click Add Questions.

Can I upload a PDF directly to SurveyMonkey?

No. SurveyMonkey does not support PDF upload as an import method. To convert a PDF to SurveyMonkey, upload the PDF to platform.piraiai.com (enable Advanced PDF Processing for scanned documents), select SurveyMonkey, and download the .txt file. Copy the content and paste into SurveyMonkey Paste Your Content.

What is SurveyMonkey Paste Your Content?

SurveyMonkey Paste Your Content (formerly Copy and Paste Questions) is the only survey import method SurveyMonkey supports. It is an AI-powered paste field powered by OpenAI and Azure OpenAI that reads pasted text, recognises question types and answer options, and creates the survey automatically. A real-time Preview panel shows how questions are detected as you paste.

Can I convert a Qualtrics QSF file to SurveyMonkey?

Yes. Upload your QSF file to platform.piraiai.com, select SurveyMonkey, and paste the formatted text output into SurveyMonkey Paste Your Content. Pirai AI maps Qualtrics question types to SurveyMonkey equivalents automatically.

Why use Pirai AI instead of copying directly from Word?

Pasting directly from Word into SurveyMonkey often fails because Word documents contain hidden formatting metadata that confuses the SurveyMonkey import. Pirai AI strips all formatting noise and generates clean structured text that SurveyMonkey's AI reads accurately. It also handles scanned PDFs, QSF files, Excel question banks, and other formats that cannot be pasted directly.
