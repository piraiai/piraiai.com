What Formswrite Does Well

Formswrite has built a strong product for the education market. With 41,000+ educators using it, the platform has genuine depth in LMS export: Canvas, Moodle, Kahoot, Quizizz, Blackboard, Schoology, Blooket, and QTI format are all supported. If your primary workflow is converting lesson materials, assessments, or quizzes into multiple LMS platforms, Formswrite's export coverage across education systems is more comprehensive than Pirai AI's.

Formswrite also offers a bulk conversion feature (multiple documents at once), a REST API for teams automating at scale, and Zapier integration on the Pro plan. For curriculum coordinators converting large batches of legacy quiz documents into Canvas or Moodle, these automation features are genuinely useful. The AI-powered question detection (Fred AI) also includes quiz and rubric generation from topics, which goes beyond pure document conversion into content creation territory.

Where Pirai AI Is the Right Fit

Your target platform is Qualtrics, REDCap, SurveyMonkey, Forsta, or another professional survey platform. Formswrite does not support any of these. Its 25+ platforms are all education and LMS platforms. If you are a market researcher, CX practitioner, clinical researcher, or survey programmer working in professional survey infrastructure, Formswrite is simply not built for your workflow.

You need to deploy the same survey to multiple professional platforms from one upload. Pirai AI's Excel intermediate layer is the key architectural difference: your document is converted to a structured, editable Excel file first. From that single Excel file, you can export to Qualtrics QSF, REDCap data dictionary, SurveyMonkey format, LimeSurvey LSS, and any other supported platform without re-uploading the original document. Edit the Excel in the AI Survey Editor - fix a question, adjust scale labels, add a block - then export to all required platforms from the updated file.

Privacy matters for your survey content. Pirai AI does not store uploaded documents. All processing happens in-memory and the document is destroyed after conversion. OpenAI API training controls are explicitly disabled, meaning no document content is used for model training. For organisations handling proprietary survey instruments, patient satisfaction surveys, or commercially sensitive research questionnaires, this architecture matters.

You use Google Forms but do not want to grant Drive access to a third party. Pirai AI's Google Forms output is a downloadable Google Apps Script file. You paste it into your own Google Apps Script environment and run it yourself. Your document never touches Pirai AI's or any third party's Google Drive connection. Formswrite creates the Google Form directly in your Drive, which requires granting Drive access to the Formswrite application.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Formswrite support Qualtrics?+

No. Formswrite supports education and LMS platforms including Google Forms, Canvas, Moodle, Kahoot, Quizizz, and Blackboard. It does not support Qualtrics, REDCap, SurveyMonkey, Forsta, or any professional survey research platform. If Qualtrics is your target, Pirai AI is the appropriate tool.

Can I use Pirai AI instead of Formswrite for Google Forms?+

Yes. Both tools support Google Forms as an output. The workflow differs: Formswrite creates the Google Form directly in your Drive (requires Drive access). Pirai AI generates a Google Apps Script file you paste and run yourself, requiring no third-party Drive access. Pirai AI also supports 26 additional professional survey platforms from the same document upload.

Does Pirai AI support Canvas or Moodle?+

Not currently. Pirai AI is focused on professional survey platforms (Qualtrics, REDCap, SurveyMonkey, Forsta, etc.) rather than LMS platforms. For Canvas and Moodle exports, Formswrite is the more appropriate tool.

What is the key architectural difference between Pirai AI and Formswrite?+

Pirai AI uses a two-step workflow: document to structured Excel (editable in the AI Survey Editor), then Excel to any number of platform outputs. The Excel intermediate layer means you convert once and can export to all supported platforms from the same file, editing between steps without re-uploading. Formswrite converts directly from document to platform output without an intermediate editable layer.
