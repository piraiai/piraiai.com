Top Forsta Alternatives for Market Researchers in 2026

Arun Raj•July 18, 2026•5 min read

Forsta (formerly Decipher, and before that FocusVision) is a professional survey platform built for market research agencies. Its strength is handling complex quantitative study designs, advanced skip logic, and multi-market deployments. Teams looking for alternatives are usually driven by one of three things: pricing, a change in team or agency structure, or a client requirement to deploy on a different platform.

What Forsta Does Well

Before evaluating alternatives, it helps to be clear about which Forsta capabilities you actually use. Forsta supports advanced scripting in a proprietary language, complex quota management, multi-language survey deployment, and detailed fieldwork controls. Not every alternative matches all of these capabilities.

Migration Considerations

Migrating from Forsta to another platform requires careful planning around three areas: survey structure, logic, and fieldwork controls.

Survey structure is the most straightforward to migrate. Question text, answer options, and recode values transfer cleanly when using Pirai AI to convert Forsta surveys to the target platform format. Pirai AI supports Forsta XML as an input format and produces QSF, QuestionPro, Alchemer Word Import, and other formats as output.

Logic is more complex. Forsta uses a proprietary scripting language for advanced conditions. Most of this logic needs to be reconstructed in the target platform's native logic editor after the structural conversion. Document all logic before beginning the migration. See the guide to preserving skip logic during migration for a structured approach.

Fieldwork controls — quota management, sample balancing, and respondent routing — are platform-specific. Confirm the target platform supports the fieldwork controls your study design requires before committing to a migration.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best alternative to Forsta for professional market research?+

The best alternative depends on your specific needs. Qualtrics is the strongest alternative for enterprise CX and complex XM programmes. UNICOM Dimensions suits teams requiring advanced conjoint and SPSS Data Collection compatibility. Alchemer is a strong mid-market option with lower cost than Forsta.

Can I migrate existing Forsta surveys to Qualtrics?+

Yes. Export your Forsta surveys in XML format, then use Pirai AI to convert them to Qualtrics QSF. The question structure and answer options transfer cleanly. Complex Forsta scripting logic needs to be reconstructed in Qualtrics branching logic after the structural import.

Does Qualtrics support the same scripting capabilities as Forsta?+

Qualtrics has advanced branching, display logic, and quota management but uses a different approach to scripting than Forsta. Forsta's proprietary scripting language has no direct equivalent in Qualtrics. Teams that rely heavily on Forsta scripting will need to redesign that logic in Qualtrics XFlow or JavaScript.

How do I choose the right Forsta alternative?+

Evaluate three factors: logic capability, platform integrations, and pricing model. If your studies use advanced conjoint or SPSS output, Dimensions is the closest match. If your priority is analytics, dashboarding, and CX programme management, Qualtrics is stronger. For mid-market research with budget constraints, Alchemer covers most research needs at a lower cost.
