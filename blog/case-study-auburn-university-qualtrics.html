The Challenge

Converting a Word survey document into a Qualtrics-ready QSF file is one of the most common bottlenecks in survey research. Question structures need to be extracted accurately. Response options need to be preserved exactly. The output needs to meet Qualtrics' specific import requirements or the file simply won't load correctly.

For Joseph Molnar, a researcher at Auburn University in the United States, finding a tool that handled all of this reliably and completely was the goal. Once he found Pirai AI, the process was exactly that.

The Solution

Joseph uploaded his Word survey document to Pirai AI. The platform extracted the full survey structure including questions, response options, question types and survey flow, then generated a clean QSF file ready for direct import into Qualtrics.
