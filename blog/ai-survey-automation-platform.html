The AI Survey Automation Platform

Arun Raj • July 18, 2026 • 2 min read

Pirai AI is the only AI-powered survey automation platform built specifically for professional researchers, survey programmers, and enterprise research teams. It converts Word, PDF, Excel, and pasted text directly into validated, platform-ready survey files — across 27+ platforms in 16 languages.

Trusted by 331 universities and research teams in 31 countries, Pirai AI closes the gap between research design and platform deployment. A 100-question survey that once took 8 to 12 hours to program manually is ready in under 15 minutes.

What Makes Pirai AI Different from General AI Survey Tools

General AI survey builders generate surveys from prompts. They are useful for quick feedback forms, NPS surveys, and simple questionnaires. Pirai AI solves a different problem: it takes an existing research document — developed through literature review, methodology design, and expert consultation — and converts it into a platform-ready file without manual programming. That distinction matters for professional researchers, CX teams, market research agencies, and healthcare teams who already have the instrument and need it deployed accurately.

The two-stage conversion architecture ensures accuracy. Stage one produces a standardised intermediate format that captures every question type, answer option, and survey structure element. Stage two converts that into the platform-specific file. The AI Survey Advisor then reviews the instrument for methodology issues before export.

Free Tools for the Qualtrics Community

Alongside the conversion platform, Pirai AI maintains a suite of nine free browser-based tools for Qualtrics practitioners. All tools run entirely client-side with no server uploads required.

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Pirai AI different from general AI survey builders?+

General AI survey builders create surveys from prompts. Pirai AI converts existing research documents into platform-ready files. This distinction matters because professional research starts with a document developed through methodology design and validation, not a prompt. Pirai AI serves that workflow rather than replacing it.

Which types of research teams use Pirai AI?+

Pirai AI is used by academic researchers converting validated instruments to REDCap and Qualtrics, market research agencies deploying client specifications to Qualtrics, Forsta, and Dimensions, CX and EX teams managing multi-platform survey programmes, healthcare researchers digitising clinical trial questionnaires, and survey programmers reducing scripting turnaround time.

Is Pirai AI secure for confidential research instruments?+

Yes. Pirai AI processes all documents 100% client-side in your browser. Your instruments never leave your device and are never uploaded to Pirai AI servers. Documents are never used to train AI models. This is a core data protection commitment that distinguishes Pirai AI from general-purpose AI tools.

How does the two-stage conversion architecture work?+

Stage one parses the source document and converts it into a standardised 7-column Excel intermediate format that captures every question type, answer option, and survey structure element. Stage two converts that intermediate format into the platform-specific file required: QSF, CSV, XML, MDD, TXT, or other formats depending on the target platform selected.
