How to Migrate from Google Forms to Qualtrics

Arun Raj • June 1, 2026 • 2 min read

Researchers and teams outgrow Google Forms when they need advanced logic, validated question types, XM analytics, or IRB-compliant data collection. Qualtrics is the natural next step. There is no direct export from Google Forms to Qualtrics, but Pirai AI converts your Google Form content into a Qualtrics QSF file in minutes.

Why Teams Migrate from Google Forms to Qualtrics

ReasonDetail
Advanced branching and logicQualtrics display logic, skip logic, and survey flow are far more powerful than Google Forms
Research complianceQualtrics supports IRB requirements, data governance, and institutional security standards
Complex question typesMatrix grids, Likert scales, rank order, and constant sum not available in Google Forms
Analytics and reportingQualtrics XM dashboards, text analytics, and cross-tab reporting
Institutional accessUniversity or enterprise Qualtrics licence requires migration from Google Forms

Step 1: Export Your Google Form Content

MethodHowBest For
Copy to Google Doc, download as .docxCopy questions to Google Docs > File > Download > Microsoft WordMost form types
Print to PDFFile > Print > Save as PDFSimple single-page forms
Response sheet as ExcelResponses tab > Link to Sheets > Download as .xlsxForms with existing response data

Step 2: Convert and Import into Qualtrics

  • 1
    Upload your exported file to Pirai AIGo to platform.piraiai.com and upload your .docx, .pdf, or .xlsx export. Select Qualtrics.
  • 2
    Download the QSF fileDownload the generated .qsf file. Pirai AI maps Google Forms question types to the correct Qualtrics equivalents.
  • 3
    Import into QualtricsIn Qualtrics, go to Create New Project > Survey > From a File. Upload the .qsf. All questions and answer options are imported automatically.
  • 4
    Add Qualtrics-specific featuresAdd display logic, embedded data, randomisation, and advanced Qualtrics features. These cannot be imported from Google Forms.
