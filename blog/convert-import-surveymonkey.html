How to Convert Word, PDF, or Excel into SurveyMonkey

Arun Raj • April 29, 2026 • 2 min read

SurveyMonkey does not accept file uploads for survey import. It uses a paste-based importer called Paste Your Content that creates questions from formatted text — with a real-time preview so you can see recognition as it happens. Pirai AI generates a .txt file formatted for SurveyMonkey's importer. Copy, paste, and your survey is built.

How SurveyMonkey Paste Your Content Works

📋 Import method: Copy & Paste via Paste Your Content

SurveyMonkey's Paste Your Content importer reads formatted text and creates Multiple Choice and Single Textbox questions automatically. A real-time Preview panel on the right shows exactly how your content is being recognised as you paste. After import you can switch question types and add further formatting in the survey editor.

What Pirai AI outputs for SurveyMonkey

A .txt file with your full survey formatted for SurveyMonkey's Paste Your Content importer. Open the file, copy all the content, and paste it on the Import Questions screen in SurveyMonkey. Click Add Questions and your survey is created instantly.

How to Convert and Import: Step by Step

  • 1
    Upload your document to Pirai AI Go to platform.piraiai.com and upload your file. Accepted: .docx .pdf .xlsx .csv .txt .xml .qsf. Or paste your survey text directly.
  • 2
    Select SurveyMonkey as your target platform Choose SurveyMonkey from the platform list. Pirai AI generates a .txt file formatted for SurveyMonkey's Paste Your Content importer.
  • 3
    Download the .txt file and copy all content Download the .txt file from Pirai AI. Open it, select all the content, and copy it to your clipboard.
  • 4
    In SurveyMonkey, click Create Survey and select Paste Your Content In SurveyMonkey, click Create Survey and select Paste Your Content from the creation options.
  • 5
    Paste your content and check the real-time preview On the Import Questions screen, paste your copied Pirai AI content. Use the Preview section on the right to check how questions are being recognised in real time as you paste.
  • 6
    Click Add Questions and review your survey Click Add Questions to create your survey. Give it a title, edit question types, and customise as needed in the SurveyMonkey survey editor.

Supported question types on import: SurveyMonkey's Paste Your Content importer recognises Multiple Choice and Single Textbox question types. After import you can edit any question, switch types, and add further formatting directly in the survey editor.
