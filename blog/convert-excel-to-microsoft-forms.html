Convert Excel to Microsoft Forms (Quick Import Does Not Support Excel)

Arun Raj • June 1, 2026 • 2 min read

Microsoft Forms Quick Import only accepts Word (.docx) and PDF files. Excel spreadsheets are not supported. If your survey, question bank, or assessment spec lives in Excel, you cannot upload it to Microsoft Forms directly. Pirai AI bridges the gap: convert your Excel file to a Microsoft Forms-ready .docx and import via Quick Import.

Why Microsoft Forms Does Not Accept Excel

Quick Import is designed to read document-style content: questions arranged vertically with answer options listed below. Excel's tabular structure does not match that format. Pirai AI reads your Excel spreadsheet, extracts the question and answer data from each row, and restructures it into a .docx formatted for Quick Import.
