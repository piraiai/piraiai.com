How to Migrate from REDCap to Qualtrics

Arun Raj • June 1, 2026 • 2 min read

REDCap excels at clinical data management, audit trails, and HIPAA compliance. Qualtrics excels at experience management, advanced survey logic, and XM analytics. When a research team needs to move instruments from REDCap into Qualtrics for broader deployment or advanced reporting, there is no direct migration path. Pirai AI converts REDCap Data Dictionary CSVs into Qualtrics QSF files.

Why Researchers Migrate from REDCap to Qualtrics

ReasonDetail
Advanced experience managementQualtrics XM features for dashboards, journey analytics, and program management
Broader deploymentQualtrics is easier to distribute to non-clinical audiences
Institutional Qualtrics accessUniversity has a Qualtrics licence and wants to consolidate platforms
Survey design flexibilityQualtrics offers more question types and display options than REDCap

Step-by-Step: REDCap to Qualtrics

  • 1
    Export your REDCap Data DictionaryIn REDCap, go to Project Setup > Data Dictionary > Download the current Data Dictionary. Save the .csv to your device.
  • 2
    Upload to Pirai AI and select QualtricsGo to platform.piraiai.com, upload your Data Dictionary .csv, and select Qualtrics. Pirai AI generates a .qsf file.
  • 3
    Import the QSF into QualtricsIn Qualtrics, go to Create New Project > Survey > From a File. Upload the .qsf. All questions and answer choices are imported automatically.
  • 4
    Review and configure in QualtricsAdd display logic, randomisation, piping, and any Qualtrics-specific features in the Qualtrics editor.

What does not migrate: REDCap branching logic, field validation rules, and calculated fields must be re-applied manually in Qualtrics after import.
