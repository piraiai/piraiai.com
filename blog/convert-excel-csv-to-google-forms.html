Convert Excel or CSV to Google Forms (Free Online Tool)

Arun Raj • May 13, 2026 • 2 min read

Researchers and educators often store survey questions in Excel spreadsheets or CSV files: question banks, codebooks, assessment specs. No competitor currently covers how to get these into Google Forms automatically. Pirai AI does.

Why Excel and CSV to Google Forms Is Different

Word documents and PDFs contain free-form text. Excel and CSV files contain structured tabular data: one column for question text, another for answer options, another for question type. Pirai AI reads this structure and maps each row to the correct Google Forms question type in the generated Apps Script.

How to Convert Excel or CSV to Google Forms

    Upload your Excel or CSV fileGo to platform.piraiai.com and upload your .xlsx or .csv file. This can be a question bank, assessment spec, survey codebook, or any structured question list.
  • 2
    Select Google FormsChoose Google Forms. Pirai AI reads the tabular structure and generates a .txt Apps Script with all questions and answer options populated.
  • 3
    Run the script in Google FormsOpen a blank Google Form. Click the three-dot menu > Apps Script. Delete default code. Paste your script. Save and Run. Grant permissions. Your form is built.
