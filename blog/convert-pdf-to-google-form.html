Convert PDF to Google Form Free (No Add-On Required)

Arun Raj • May 13, 2026 • 2 min read

Google Forms has no PDF upload option. The only way to get a PDF survey or quiz into Google Forms without re-typing everything is to convert the PDF first. Pirai AI converts any PDF into a Google Apps Script that builds your form automatically. No add-on installation. No Google Workspace required.

Why You Should Not Use a Google Workspace Add-On

Tools like Automagical Forms require you to install a Google Workspace Marketplace add-on, grant permissions to your Google Drive, and run inside Google Drive. If your institution restricts Workspace Marketplace installs, or you prefer not to give third-party add-ons access to your Drive, this is a problem.

Pirai AI is web-based. You upload your PDF at platform.piraiai.com, download an Apps Script file, and paste it into Google Forms. No Workspace add-on. No Drive access permissions required.

How to Convert PDF to Google Form

  • 1
    Upload your PDF to Pirai AIGo to platform.piraiai.com and upload your PDF. Works for native PDFs, scanned documents, and paper questionnaires scanned to PDF.
  • 2
    Select Google FormsChoose Google Forms. Pirai AI extracts all questions and answer options from the PDF and generates a .txt Apps Script.
  • 3
    Run the script in Google FormsOpen a blank Google Form. Click the three-dot menu > Apps Script. Delete default code. Paste your script. Click Save then Run. All questions are created automatically.
