How to Migrate from Qualtrics to QuestionPro Without Losing Survey Structure

Arun Raj•July 18, 2026•4 min read

Teams migrate from Qualtrics to QuestionPro for a range of reasons: licensing costs, institutional procurement decisions, feature requirements, or a preference for QuestionPro's pricing model. Whatever the reason, the migration challenge is the same: getting existing surveys out of Qualtrics and into QuestionPro without rebuilding every question manually.

Why Teams Migrate from Qualtrics to QuestionPro

Qualtrics is an enterprise platform with enterprise pricing. For academic institutions, small research teams, and independent researchers, the cost of a Qualtrics licence can be prohibitive when a QuestionPro licence covers the same survey types at a lower cost. QuestionPro also offers academic discounts and a research-focused tier that appeals to university teams.

Some teams migrate after Qualtrics changes its pricing structure or removes features from lower licence tiers. Others migrate when an institutional agreement lapses and is not renewed. The surveys do not disappear — but getting them into the new platform requires work.

The Migration Process

1

Export your Qualtrics survey as a QSF file

In Qualtrics, go to Survey and select Tools, then Import/Export Survey, then Export Survey. Download the QSF file to your device.

2

Convert with Pirai AI

Upload the QSF file to platform.piraiai.com and select QuestionPro as the target platform. Pirai AI converts the Qualtrics survey structure into a QuestionPro-compatible format, mapping question types and preserving answer option structure.

3

Import into QuestionPro

Follow the QuestionPro import process using the converted file. Review the imported survey against your QSF original to confirm question text and answer options are accurate.

4

Rebuild logic and flow

Using your pre-migration logic documentation as reference, add skip logic and display conditions in QuestionPro. Test every logic path before activating the survey.

Document your Qualtrics surveys before migration

Use the QSF to Excel Converter to export your Qualtrics survey into a structured Excel workbook before migration begins. This gives you a complete reference of every question, answer option, recode value, and logic condition in the original survey — useful for validation after the QuestionPro import.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I import a QSF file directly into QuestionPro?+

No. QuestionPro does not accept QSF files natively. The QSF must first be converted to a QuestionPro-compatible format using Pirai AI, then imported via QuestionPro's import function.

What happens to Qualtrics embedded data during migration to QuestionPro?+

Qualtrics embedded data does not have a direct equivalent in QuestionPro. You will need to set up QuestionPro custom variables to replicate the embedded data functionality after the survey structure has been imported.

How long does it take to migrate a Qualtrics survey to QuestionPro?+

Using Pirai AI, the structural conversion from QSF to QuestionPro format takes minutes. Adding logic and custom variables in QuestionPro after import depends on the complexity of the original survey, typically one to three hours for a 50 to 100 question study with moderate logic.

Does skip logic migrate automatically from Qualtrics to QuestionPro?+

Basic skip logic migrates with review required. Complex nested branching and embedded data conditions need to be reconstructed in QuestionPro's logic editor after the structural import.
