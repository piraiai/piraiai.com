ChatGPT to Qualtrics in Minutes: How Pirai AI Is Changing the Survey Workflow

Arun Raj • March 27, 2026 • 6 min read

Survey researchers have always had two separate worlds. The creative world of designing questions and the technical world of programming them into platforms. For years, these two worlds stayed apart. Researchers designed. Programmers built. Time was lost in between.

AI changed the first part. ChatGPT made it incredibly easy to brainstorm, draft, and refine survey questions in seconds. But the second part, getting those questions into a platform like Qualtrics, was still a manual, time-consuming process.

Until now.

The Old Way

A researcher uses ChatGPT to generate a 10-question community engagement survey. The questions look great. The structure is solid. Now what?

They copy the text into a Word document. Reformat it. Send it to a programmer. The programmer manually builds it in Qualtrics, one question at a time, setting question types, adding answer options, configuring recode values. Hours pass.

This is the gap between AI-assisted research design and platform-ready deployment. Pirai AI was built to close it.

What Makes This Possible

At the heart of Pirai AI is a two-stage conversion architecture.

Stage 1: Universal Extraction

Takes your input, whether it is pasted text, a Word document, a PDF, or a spreadsheet, and converts it into a standardised 7-column Excel format. This is the universal intermediate layer that understands every question type, every answer option, and every survey structure.

→

Stage 2: Platform Export

Takes that standardised Excel and converts it into the platform-specific format of your choice. Qualtrics QSF. QuestionPro. REDCap. SurveyMonkey. Microsoft Forms. Google Forms. And 15+ more. A clean, validated, platform-ready file every time.
