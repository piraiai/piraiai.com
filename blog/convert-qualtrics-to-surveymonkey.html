Convert Qualtrics Survey to SurveyMonkey

Arun Raj • May 29, 2026 • 2 min read

Migrating from Qualtrics to SurveyMonkey is common when institutional licences change or teams switch platforms. There is no direct export-import path between the two platforms. Pirai AI bridges the gap: export your Qualtrics survey as a QSF file, convert it with Pirai AI, and paste the result into SurveyMonkey.

Why Researchers Migrate from Qualtrics to SurveyMonkey

ReasonDetail
CostSurveyMonkey has more accessible pricing for small teams
Licence changeInstitutional Qualtrics licence not renewed
Team preferenceSurveyMonkey is more familiar to non-research stakeholders
DistributionSurveyMonkey has built-in panel and audience tools

Step-by-Step: Qualtrics to SurveyMonkey

  • 1
    Export your Qualtrics survey as a QSF fileIn Qualtrics, go to Survey > Tools > Import/Export Survey > Export Survey. Download the .qsf file to your device.
  • 2
    Upload the QSF to Pirai AIGo to platform.piraiai.com, upload your .qsf file, and select SurveyMonkey as the output platform.
  • 3
    Download the .txt file and copy all contentDownload the Pirai AI generated .txt file. Open it, select all, and copy.
  • 4
    In SurveyMonkey, create a survey using Paste Your ContentGo to Create Survey > Paste Your Content. Paste your copied content. Review the real-time preview. Click Add Questions.

What does not migrate: Qualtrics display logic, branching, embedded data, quota settings, and randomisation must be manually re-applied in SurveyMonkey after import. SurveyMonkey's Paste Your Content also does not capture page breaks.
