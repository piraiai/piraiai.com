How to Convert a Document to Google Forms

  • 1
    Upload your document to Pirai AIGo to platform.piraiai.com. Upload your file or paste text directly. Accepted formats: .docx .pdf .xlsx .csv .txt .xml .qsf .json. For scanned paper surveys, enable Advanced PDF Processing.
  • 2
    Select Google Forms and download the scriptChoose Google Forms as the output platform. Pirai AI converts your document to a structured survey draft and generates a .txt Apps Script file with all questions and answer options pre-populated.
  • 3
    Paste and run in Google FormsOpen a blank Google Form. Click the three-dot menu (top right) and select Apps Script. Delete the default code. Paste your script. Click Save then Run. Grant the one-time Google permission. Your complete form is built automatically.

Total time: under 5 minutes

Upload to Pirai AI: 30 seconds. Processing: 30-60 seconds. Paste and run in Google Forms: 2 minutes including the permissions grant. The permissions grant is a one-time Google safety check : it is not specific to Pirai AI and does not grant any third party access to your account.
