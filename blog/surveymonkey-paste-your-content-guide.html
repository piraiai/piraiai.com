SurveyMonkey Paste Your Content: Complete Guide to Importing Questions

Arun Raj • May 29, 2026 • 2 min read

SurveyMonkey's Paste Your Content feature lets you import a survey from any text source by pasting it into an AI-powered field. It recognises question types automatically and builds the survey for you. This guide explains exactly how it works, what its limits are, and how to get the best results when converting from Word, PDF, or Excel.

How SurveyMonkey Paste Your Content Works

Paste Your Content is powered by OpenAI/Azure OpenAI. When you paste survey text, SurveyMonkey's AI reads the structure and identifies question types, answer options, and response formats. A real-time Preview panel on the right shows how the content is being detected as you paste : before you commit to creating the survey.

How to access Paste Your Content

In SurveyMonkey, click Create Survey. Select Paste Your Content as the starting point and click Create Survey. Agree to use AI. Paste your survey text into the Import Questions screen. Review the Preview panel. Click Add Questions.
