Convert Word Doc to Google Form Without Losing Formatting

Arun Raj • May 13, 2026 • 2 min read

When you copy a Word document and paste it into Google Forms manually, formatting collapses. Multiple choice options turn into plain text. Matrix grids disappear. Headers become regular paragraphs. The structure you spent time building is gone.

Pirai AI preserves your survey structure by reading the document intelligently and converting each element to the correct Google Forms question type before the import happens.

How Pirai AI Preserves Formatting

Pirai AI reads your Word document and identifies each question type based on structure and context. It then generates a Google Apps Script that creates each question in Google Forms with the correct type already set. You do not need to manually select question types after import.
