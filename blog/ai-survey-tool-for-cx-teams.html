AI Survey Tool for CX and EX Teams

Arun Raj • July 18, 2026 • 2 min read

CX and EX teams manage ongoing survey programmes across multiple platforms, environments, and regions. When programmes need updating, migrating between platforms, or deploying to new geographies, the manual rebuild work adds up fast.

Pirai AI converts existing survey documents into any platform format and gives CX and EX teams a suite of free tools to document, audit, and compare workflow configurations across environments — all in the browser, with no uploads required.

Workflow Documentation for CX Teams

CX programmes running on Qualtrics often involve complex workflow configurations — Dashboard data mappings, XM Directory contact field mappings, and workflow task configurations that need to be documented before migrations or handovers. The free Qualtrics Toolkit includes browser-based extractor scripts that pull the complete configuration of any workflow task into a CSV in seconds.

The Dashboard Field Mapper documents all field types and data source mappings from a Qualtrics Dashboard. The XM Directory Mapper extracts all contact and transaction field mappings from the Update XM Directory Contacts task. Both tools are free, client-side, and require no installation.

Environment Comparison Before Deployment

Configuration drift between Dev, Test, and Production environments is one of the most common causes of CX programme failures that are difficult to diagnose. Extract configurations from each environment using the free mapper tools, then upload the CSVs to the Pirai AI CSV Comparator to instantly identify differences. The AI Survey Advisor reviews every survey instrument for methodology and wording issues before respondents see it.
