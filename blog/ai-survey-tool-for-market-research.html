AI Survey Tool for Market Research Teams

Arun Raj • July 18, 2026 • 2 min read

Market research teams spend more time programming surveys than analysing them. A client delivers a Word specification, and a programmer rebuilds it manually inside Qualtrics, Forsta, Dimensions, or Alchemer — one question at a time. For a complex quantitative study, that is hours of work before a single response is collected.

Pirai AI converts that specification directly into a platform-ready import file. The same document can be deployed to multiple platforms without rebuilding anything, giving agencies and in-house teams true zero vendor lock-in.

Platform Coverage for Market Research

Market research agencies work across a wider range of platforms than most sectors. A tracker might run on Qualtrics. A custom study might go on Forsta. An FMCG client might require Dimensions MDD. An emerging market project might use a regional platform. Rebuilding the same specification for each platform manually is one of the most significant cost drains in research operations.

Pirai AI supports Qualtrics QSF, Forsta XML, UNICOM Dimensions MDD, Alchemer Word Import format, SurveyMonkey TXT, and 22+ more from a single upload. The same client specification produces every output format without reformatting. For teams working across environments, the Pirai AI CSV Comparator adds instant configuration comparison across Dev, Test, and Production.

Methodology Review Before Fieldwork

Client questionnaires arrive in all states of polish. Leading questions, inconsistent scale labels, and answer options that do not belong on a rating scale are common. The AI Survey Advisor reviews the instrument before deployment, flagging Critical, Important, and Minor issues with exact edit instructions. For market research teams, that is a QC layer that previously required a senior methodologist to review manually.
