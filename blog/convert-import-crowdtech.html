How to Convert Word, PDF, or Excel into Crowdtech

Arun Raj • April 29, 2026 • 2 min read

Crowdtech imports surveys through its Download Center using a native .ctsurvey file format. Once uploaded, you choose exactly which survey elements to bring in — from question text through to quotas, translations, and filters. Pirai AI generates the .ctsurvey file from any source document so your survey is ready to import in minutes.

How Crowdtech Survey Import Works

⬆ Import method: File Upload via Download Center

Crowdtech's Download Center handles both exporting and importing surveys. To import, navigate to Import Survey within the Survey environment you want to populate, upload your .ctsurvey file, and select which elements to include. The system auto-selects Question and Answer Texts but gives you full control over optional elements.

What Pirai AI outputs for Crowdtech

A .ctsurvey file containing your full survey in Crowdtech's native import format. Upload it via Download Center > Import Survey, select your elements, enter a name, choose a layout, and click Import.

Selectable Import Elements

Crowdtech gives you granular control over what gets imported from the .ctsurvey file. Here is a full reference of what you can include:

ElementAuto-selectedDescription
Question and Answer TextsYesAll question content and answer options — always included
QuotasOptionalQuota definitions and limits for the survey
Data enrichment (column names)OptionalCustom variable names for data export
Confirmation messagesOptionalEnd-of-survey and thank-you messages
FiltersOptionalDisplay and routing filters
VariablesOptionalCustom variables used in the survey
GroupsOptionalRespondent groups defined in the survey
EmailsOptionalEmail templates associated with the survey
TranslationsOptionalLanguage translations included in the file
