CSV Comparator: Compare Two CSV Files Online Free

Arun Raj • June 1, 2026 • 2 min read

Comparing two CSV files manually is painful. Thousands of rows, multiple columns, no easy way to see what changed, what is missing, or what does not match. Pirai AI's free CSV Comparator finds every difference, duplicate, and mismatch between two CSV files : handling up to 100,000 rows, entirely in your browser. No file uploads. No data leaves your device.

How to Compare Two CSV Files

  • 1
    Open the CSV ComparatorGo to piraiai.com/pirai-csv-comparator (web version) or install the Chrome extension for use directly in your browser.
  • 2
    Load your two CSV filesSelect or drag and drop your first CSV file into the left panel and your second CSV file into the right panel. Files are processed locally : nothing is uploaded.
  • 3
    Review the comparison resultsThe tool shows differences side by side: rows only in File A, rows only in File B, duplicate rows, and field structure differences between the two files.
  • 4
    Export the difference reportDownload a detailed difference report as CSV for further analysis or to share with your team.
