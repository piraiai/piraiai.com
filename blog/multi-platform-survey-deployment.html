Multi-Platform Survey Deployment

Arun Raj • July 18, 2026 • 2 min read

When a survey needs to run on multiple platforms — or migrate from one platform to another — teams traditionally rebuild it from scratch in each destination. For a large instrument, that means days of duplicated work, multiple rounds of QA, and the risk of inconsistencies appearing between platform versions.

Pirai AI converts a single document into any supported platform format. Upload once, select the target platform, and download the validated import file. The same instrument can be deployed to Qualtrics today and REDCap tomorrow without rebuilding anything.

Why Multi-Platform Deployment Matters

Survey programmes rarely live on a single platform for their entire lifecycle. A clinical instrument might start in REDCap for data collection and need to move to Qualtrics for broader deployment. A market research tracker running on Qualtrics might need to be replicated on Forsta for a specific client. An academic survey designed for Google Forms might need to move to REDCap for HIPAA-compliant data handling.

Each of these migrations traditionally requires a programmer to rebuild the instrument from scratch in the destination platform — question by question, answer option by answer option. Pirai AI removes that rebuild step entirely. The original document becomes the single source of truth, and the platform file is generated on demand.
